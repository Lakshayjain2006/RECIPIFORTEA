|recipi for tea|

item required

  1. water
  2. sugar
  3. milk
  4. tea leaves
  5. ginger
  6. black paper
  7. elaichi
  8. long

Procedure

1. Put water in a pan and let it be warm.
2. After when water start boiling put the tea leaves in it.
3. Grill all the materials together rather than milk
4. Now add the powdered materials in the water
5. When mixture became red or black. put milk in it
6. Now boil the mixture 3-4 times
7. Now the tea is ready to serve.
        
tips for better taste

        1. Use fresh milk.
        2. Use fresh ginger.
        3. Make sure the ratio of milk and water.
        4. Serve the tea in chinese utensil.
        5. Serve tea with some light snackS. like biscuit etc.
        6. Make sure that the tea with proper deserving manner will help you to make it better.
